Introduction

The Happiest people I know are those who lose themselves in the service of Others.

Unity (A Group of Social Work) is a great platform to serve the society through donating clothes, blood, teaching poor children, donating food and such donations which helps the needy.





Aim

Our aim is to find people who are willing to do social work. We all know that some people want to do social service, but they are suppressed because of being alone. That's why our aim is to serve the society together, so that all of us together can face any problem.





Offer

Unity Group offers you to join and work with us, this is a wonderful opportunity to serve the society well. We are always with you and stand by your problems. We are looking for honest people. At present our team works in Delhi, Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Punjab, Jammu, Himachal and some areas of Assam.
